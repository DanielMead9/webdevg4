Our Tax Preparation Process

We've streamlined our tax preparation process to make it as simple and stress-free as possible for you.

1

Initial Consultation

We start with a complimentary consultation to understand your tax situation, review your documents, and explain our services. This can be done in-person, over the phone, or via video call.

2

Document Collection

We'll provide you with a comprehensive checklist of documents needed. You can submit them securely through our portal, email, or bring them to our office. Common documents include W-2s, 1099s, receipts, and prior year returns.

3

Preparation & Review

Our tax professionals will carefully prepare your return, ensuring all deductions and credits are applied. We'll conduct a thorough review to ensure accuracy and compliance with current tax laws.

4

Your Review & Approval

We'll schedule a meeting to walk you through your completed return, answer any questions, and explain the results. You'll review everything before we file.

5

E-Filing & Follow-up

Once approved, we'll electronically file your return for the fastest processing. We'll provide you with copies for your records and remain available for any questions or IRS correspondence.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start preparing my taxes?
We recommend starting as soon as you receive all your tax documents, typically by late January or early February. This gives us plenty of time to prepare an accurate return and helps you receive any refund faster. However, we accept clients through the April deadline and can file extensions if needed.
How long does the tax preparation process take?
For most individual returns, we can complete preparation within 3-7 business days once we have all your documents. Business and corporate returns may take 1-2 weeks depending on complexity. We also offer expedited service for urgent situations.
Can you help if I'm being audited by the IRS?
Yes! We provide IRS representation services and can help you navigate an audit. If we prepared your original return, we'll stand behind our work. We can also assist with audits of returns prepared by others.
What if I haven't filed taxes in several years?
We can help you get caught up. We'll work with you to prepare back returns and develop a strategy to resolve any outstanding tax liabilities. The sooner you address this, the better—contact us for a confidential consultation.
Do you offer year-round tax planning?
Absolutely! Tax planning shouldn't be a once-a-year activity. We offer quarterly check-ins, estimated tax payment calculations, and strategic planning to help minimize your tax liability throughout the year.
How do I securely share my documents with you?
We take your privacy seriously. You can submit documents via our secure client portal, encrypted email, or bring physical copies to our office. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your sensitive information.
